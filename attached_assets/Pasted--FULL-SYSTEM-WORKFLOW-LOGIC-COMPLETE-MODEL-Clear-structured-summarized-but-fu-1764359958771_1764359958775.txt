This is the **heart** of the system. Everything is linked to map operations.

### **3.1 Map Loading**

* Loads **offline map tiles**

### **3.2 Adding Network Elements**

### **3.3 Editing Elements**

When placing two points:

* System calculates fiber distance
* Stores it as part of route
* Updates route summary automatically
